Dalyan Kofte

Ingredients:

For Meatballs;
500 g of medium-fat ground beef,
1 onion,
3 tablespoons of breadcrumbs,
1 egg yolk and half of 1 egg white,
Cumin,
Black pepper,
Salt.

For topping;
Half of 1 egg white.

For the sauce;
5-6 tablespoons of olive oil,
1 heaping tablespoon of tomato paste,
1.5 cups of water,
Salt.

For the filling;
3 eggs,
700 g canned mixed vegetables.

Preparation:

1)Boil the eggs in solid form for the filling,
2)For the meatballs, knead the ground beef, egg yolk and half of the egg white, grated onion,
breadcrumbs, cumin, black pepper and salt in a deep bowl,
3)Spread greaseproof paper on a baking tray,
4)Spread the meatball mixture in a rectangular shape on the wax paper,
5)Sprinkle half of the canned vegetables on top,
6)Place the eggs in the middle,
7)Fold the meatloaf with the support of greaseproof paper from both sides and join it in the middle,
8)Spread the half egg white on top,
9)Bake in a 200 C degrees preheated oven golden brown,
10)For the sauce, heat the olive oil in a pan and add the tomato paste and stir fry until smells good,
11)Add the water and salt and mix,
12)Simmer for a few mins. and remove from the heat,
13)Transfer the meatloaf into a properly sized ovenware,
14)Pour the sauce over,
15)Place the remaining canned mix vegetables in two sides,
16)Return it back to the oven and bake for 10 mins. more.
